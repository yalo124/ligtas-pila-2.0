Stay Home, Stay Safe:

Aid Distribution Without the Wait.

Ligtas-Pila is a digital system designed to improve the distribution. The system helps remove long physical queues during aid distribution. It also protects vulnerable people such as senior citizens, PWDs, and low-income families from heat, exhaustion, and health risks. Through remote processing, assistance can be delivered faster, safer, and more organized.

About Us

Ligtas-Pila is a non-governmental organization dedicated to restoring dignity, safety, and efficiency to public aid distribution. Born from the urgent need to protect vulnerable sectors such as low-income families, senior citizens, and persons with disabilities (PWDs) from the health risks of extreme weather and grueling physical lines, we leverage data and logic to transform how communities receive support.

Form Completion Guide: Step-by-Step Instructions

Welcome! Please follow these instructions carefully to ensure your application is processed without any delays.


Step 1: Personal Information

In the Name section, please break down your full name into the specific fields provided:

  • Enter your Last Name, First Name, and Middle Name in their respective boxes.
  • If you have a Suffix (such as Jr., Sr., or III), enter it in the suffix field. If not, leave it blank.

Step 2: Address Details

Provide your current residential address. Select or type the following in order:

  • Region
  • City
  • District
  • Barangay

Step 3: Contact Information

We need a way to reach you regarding your application status. Please provide:

  • An active mobile or landline Number.
  • A valid Email Address that you check regularly.

Step 4: Monthly Income Range

  • Select or enter your Monthly Income. Please ensure this reflects your current gross monthly earnings.

Step 5: Select Your Condition / Category

Choose the specific option that applies to your current situation. You may select from:

  • Unemployed
  • Transport Worker
  • Disaster-Affected
  • Senior Citizen
  • PWD (Person with Disability)
  • Medical Assistance
  • Educational Assistance
  • Burial Assistance

Important Note on Document Uploads:

  • If you selected Medical Assistance, you are required to upload a clear photo or PDF of your Medical Bill
  • If you selected Educational Assistance, you are required to upload a clear photo or PDF of your Tuition Fee Assessment.
  • If you selected Burial Assistance, you are required to upload a clear photo or PDF of the Death Certificate.

Step 6: Valid ID Upload

  • To verify your identity, please upload a digital copy (photo or scanned file) of one Valid ID. Ensure that your name, photo, and birthdate are clearly visible and not blurry.

Final Step: Review and Submit

Before clicking submit, please double-check all your entries and uploaded documents. Once everything is verified, click Submit.
